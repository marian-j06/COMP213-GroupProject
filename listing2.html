Listing Description

This custom modern home offers 2,500–3,000 sq. ft. of luxury living in the prestigious Edenbridge–Humber Valley community. Located on one of the area’s best streets and backing onto St. George’s Golf Course, this architectural home features floor-to-ceiling Pella windows, white oak engineered hardwood, and premium Gaggenau appliances. The main level is bright and contemporary with heated bathroom floors, quartz finishes, and custom cabinetry throughout. Smart-home automation with three climate zones, a heated driveway and steps, and views shaded by mature Serbian pines add comfort and privacy. The home includes 3+1 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, and an attached garage, making it a rare modern retreat in one of Toronto’s most sought-after neighbourhoods.
