Listing Description

This stunning custom-built home blends modern style with calm, Zen-inspired design. It offers over 6,600 sq. ft. of finished living space, thoughtfully built over four years.
You’re welcomed by a dramatic 20-foot foyer with a glowing glass pivot door. The main floor is bright and open, featuring a floating staircase, feature walls, and warm custom woodwork. The family room includes a two-sided linear fireplace, and the executive office has custom built-ins with beautiful treetop views.
The chef’s kitchen is a highlight, with high-end Miele appliances, a PITT gas cooktop built into the counter, and a separate catering kitchen. Skylights, heated floors, stylish cabinetry, and a modern powder room complete the main level.
The primary suite is a private retreat with a spa-like ensuite, custom walnut-and-glass walk-in closets, and a cozy lounge area with a 3-sided fireplace. The additional bedrooms each have their own ensuite bathrooms and built-in cabinetry. A flexible fourth bedroom can be used however you need. The upper level also includes a fully customized laundry room.
The lower level is designed for entertainment and wellness. It features a 15-seat soundproof theatre, a glass-walled climate-controlled wine room, an open gym, and a modern wet bar. There’s also a nanny or guest suite with a spa-like ensuite and direct walkout to the landscaped backyard.
Outside, the home has modern brick and aluminum finishes, professional landscaping with irrigation, and sleek exterior lighting. Smart home technology is built in throughout, using a Control4 system for easy control of lights, temperature, and more. This home is a true modern Zen retreat—combining design, comfort, technology, and tranquility in one exceptional space.
